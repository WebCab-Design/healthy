Dr. Bongi is a Licensed Naturopathic Primary Care Doctor in the State of Arizona. She was recruited to work in the USA in 1995 and has 19 years of experience in rehabilitation, specializing in neurologic and orthopedic conditions in hospital, out patient and long term care facilities. Dr Bongi is the founder of Healthy Living and Primary Care Clinic in Tucson, AZ. She is currently a member of the Association for the Advancements of Restorative Medicine (AARM).

Throughout her work in the medical field, Dr Bongi recognized the stress on individuals and their families when dealing with debilitating neurologic and autoimmune conditions such as Parkinson Disease, Rheumatoid Arthritis, Strokes and numerous other chronic conditions. She is a strong advocate for prevention and management of disease through healthy life style, nutrition and more natural therapies. Through training and clinical experience, she seeks to integrate treatments for a better outcomes.
